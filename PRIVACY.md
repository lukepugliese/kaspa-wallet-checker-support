# Privacy Policy – Kaspa Wallet Checker

Last updated: 2026-07-04

Kaspa Wallet Checker is a read-only lookup tool for the Kaspa (KAS) blockchain.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. The app does not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information.

When you enter a wallet address, the app queries the public Kaspa network API directly from your device to display balance and transaction information. This request is not linked to your identity and is not stored by us.

## Third-Party Services

The app communicates directly with the public Kaspa blockchain API (api.kaspa.org) to retrieve publicly available blockchain data. No other third-party services are used.
